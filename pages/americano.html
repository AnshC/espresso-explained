How To Americano.

  • Brew 1 or 2 shots (1 to 2 ounces) of espresso.
  • Boil water and pour 6 to 8 ounces into a cup.
  • Gently add the espresso to the hot water, preserving the crema.
  • Serve immediately for a smooth, rich experience.

Americano is a coffee drink made by diluting espresso with hot water, resulting in a smooth, mild flavor while retaining the rich depth of espresso. Its larger serving size and lighter taste make it a versatile choice, perfect for those who enjoy a more subtle coffee experience.

The Americano is said to have originated during World War II when American soldiers in Italy diluted espresso to mimic the coffee they were accustomed to back home. Today, the Americano is a staple in coffee shops worldwide, celebrated for its simplicity and adaptability.
