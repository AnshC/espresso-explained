Latte Explained.

Latte: Espresso with steamed milk, topped with foam.

Smooth Comfort in Every Sip

How To Latte.

  • Brew 1 shot (1 ounce) of espresso.
  • Steam 6 to 8 ounces of milk until frothy and velvety.
  • Pour the steamed milk over the espresso, holding back the foam.
  • Top with a small layer of foam and optionally create latte art.

Latte is a creamy and indulgent coffee drink made with a shot of espresso and a generous amount of steamed milk, topped with a thin layer of foam. Its silky texture and subtle flavor make it a favorite for coffee lovers seeking a mellow yet satisfying beverage. Often served in larger cups, latte is perfect for savoring slowly and pairing with a variety of flavors.
