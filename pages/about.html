The Coffee Dictionary

Welcome to Espresso Explained. This website is a small repository of standard coffee drinks you may encounter in a cafe. You can find definitions, recipes, and images of these drinks in their individual pages, alongside a breif explanation of the drink and where it originated from.

This website was inspired by the book The Coffee Dictionary by Maxwell Colonna-Dashwood. As an avid coffee enthusiast, I designed and developed this website for people trying to get into coffee.
