How To Drip Coffee.

  • Use 1 to 2 tablespoons of ground coffee per 6 ounces of water.
  • Place a paper or reusable filter in the drip coffee maker.
  • Add water to the reservoir and start the brewing cycle.
  • Once brewed, pour and enjoy the smooth, classic flavor.

Drip coffee is a traditional brewing method where hot water is poured over coffee grounds, filtered, and collected into a carafe or cup. Known for its smooth and consistent taste, it is one of the most popular ways to make coffee at home or in cafes around the world.

The roots of drip coffee trace back to Germany, where Melitta Bentz invented the first coffee filter in 1908. Her innovation revolutionized coffee brewing by making it more accessible and efficient. Today, drip coffee remains a beloved staple for its convenience and reliable flavor, symbolizing comfort and tradition in every cup.
