How To Make a Red Eye.

  • Brew a regular cup of drip coffee (6-8 ounces).
  • Prepare a single shot of espresso (1 ounce).
  • Pour the espresso shot into the brewed coffee.
  • Stir and serve immediately for a powerful pick-me-up.

A Red Eye is a coffee drink that combines regular drip coffee with a shot of espresso for an added caffeine kick. It’s perfect for those needing a strong jolt of energy without sacrificing the taste of classic brewed coffee.

The Red Eye is thought to have originated as a quick solution for those needing a boost during long shifts or travel. Today, it’s a favorite for coffee lovers who want to combine the smoothness of drip coffee with the intensity of espresso, creating a unique and energizing drink.
